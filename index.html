Interests & Hobbies

Blockchain Technology

 

I love blockchain technology as this innovation has completely blown me away with its ability to solve many of the unsolvable problems we have today. In March of 2021 I read the bitcoin whitepaper where I learnd that bitcoin is a “peer-to-peer network [that] timestamps transactions by hashing them into an ongoing chain of hash-based proof-of-work, forming a record that cannot be changed without redoing the proof-of-work” (Nakamoto, 2008). Immediatly I realized the potential for such a technology to revolutionize various industries improving their inclusivness, speed, relyability, and security. Around the same time I realized that, while bitcoin is a great proof of concept it is in no way scalable, the more users it has the worse the network performs so I knew there was still a lot of work to do to make these blockchains actually useful, but once the frameworks are inplace and the laws catch up, this tech has limitless potential.

For a detailed look into how blockchain works and the specific problems with bitcoin please read my full article.
